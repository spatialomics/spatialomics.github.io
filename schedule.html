About

We are a dedicated team of Ph.D. and Master's students from DKFZ Heidelberg, organizing the Spverse hackathon under the expert guidance of Prof. Oliver Stegle and Prof. Moritz Gerstung. Our aim is to bring together passionate individuals and foster innovation in the field of spatial omics analysis. If you are interested in joining us or have any inquiries, please don't hesitate to contact us via email. We look forward to hearing from you and collaborating to push the boundaries of spatial omics research.
